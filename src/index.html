How to Play

  1. Move your car using WASD or the arrow keys
  2. Honk using the spacebar
  3. Hold down left click and move your mouse to rotate the camera
  4. Hold down right click and move your mouse to move the camera
  5. Use your scroll wheel to zoom in or out

Some objects in the scene are interactable! When your car approaches them, you will be notified with a popup.

This is an early demo of the simulator! If you face issues with the car getting stuck, please refresh the page. You can also refresh the page to restart the simulator.
